Privacy Policy

Who We Are

We are HealthCare Plus, committed to delivering high-quality healthcare services to individuals and families. Our website address is HealthCare Plus.

What Personal Data We Collect and Why

Comments

When visitors leave comments on the site, we collect the data shown in the comments form, along with the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent string. This information helps us identify and respond to comments effectively, as well as prevent spam.

An anonymized hash created from your email address might be provided to the Gravatar service to check if you use it. Review the Gravatar service privacy policy for details. After your comment is approved, your profile picture becomes visible to the public alongside your comment.

Media

If you upload images to the website, please be aware that including embedded location data (EXIF GPS) in the images is discouraged. Visitors can download and extract location data from website images.

Contact Forms

We use contact forms to facilitate communication with you. The data collected may include your name, email address, and any information you choose to share. We collect this data to respond to your inquiries, provide personalized support, and improve our services.

Cookies

When you interact with our site, certain information is stored in cookies to enhance your browsing experience. For example, if you leave a comment on our site, you can opt to save your name, email address, and website in cookies for convenience. These cookies last for one year. If you have an account and log in, temporary cookies are used to maintain your login status. These cookies are discarded upon closing your browser.

Additionally, logging in sets additional cookies to save login information and screen display choices. Login cookies are stored for two days, and screen options cookies for a year. Selecting “Remember Me” extends your login persistence to two weeks. Logging out removes login cookies. Editing or publishing an article saves an additional cookie in your browser, which contains no personal data and expires after 1 day.

Embedded Content from Other Websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g., videos, images, articles) from third-party websites. Embedded content from other websites behaves in the same way as if the visitor visited those websites directly. These websites may collect data, use cookies, embed third-party tracking, and monitor interactions with embedded content, including when logged in to their website.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze and track user behavior on our website. This helps us understand how visitors interact with our site, allowing us to improve our content and user experience. The data collected is anonymized and used for statistical purposes only.

Who We Share Your Data With

We prioritize the protection of your personal data and do not share it with third parties unless necessary for the operation of our website and services. Trusted services integral to our website's operation may have access to your personal data, such as hosting providers and payment gateways.

How Long We Retain Your Data

Comments and their metadata are retained indefinitely for follow-up comment recognition. For registered users, personal information in user profiles is stored. All users can view, edit, or delete their personal information. Website administrators can also see and edit this information.

Your Rights Over Your Data

If you have an account or have left comments on our site, you have the right to request an exported file of your personal data or request data erasure. However, please note that we may retain certain data for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where We Send Your Data

Your comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service. We utilize secure and trusted third-party services for efficient data handling and processing, ensuring the protection of your personal data.
